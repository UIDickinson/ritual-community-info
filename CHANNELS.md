# Ritual Discord — Channels Guide

| # | Channel | Purpose |
|---|---------|---------|
| 1 | **Announcements** | Official announcements from the Ritual Team. |
| 2 | **Events** | Strictly for community event announcements. |
| 3 | **Rules** | Ritual rules to live by. In short: don't be a jerk and don't spam. |
| 4 | **Blessings of Syn** | For the Ritual pledge. |
| 5 | **Updates** | All updates in the community. |
| 6 | **Gritual** | Channel for Ritual greetings ("gritual!"). |
| 7 | **Community** | Discussion on everything, including off-topic. All server rules apply. |
| 8 | **Contributions** | Share your Ritual guides, tutorials, and explainers (blog posts, videos, documentation). |
| 9 | **Confessions** | Confess your syns. |
| 10 | **Food** | Post your food! Delicious dishes from at-home chefs or great restaurants. |
| 11 | **Ritual** | The general Ritual chat. Talk about Ritual Chain, AI, Crypto. Ask questions. Share news at the intersection of AI × Crypto. |
| 12 | **Vestibule** | Invoke blessings or curses on other community members. |
| 13 | **Report** | Report bots and scams. Do NOT open a ticket with the support system here. |
| 14 | **Rank** | Check your rank in the Ritual community. |
| 15 | **Wen** | Community members say "wen." (as in "wen token?", "wen airdrop?") |
| 16 | **Nominations** | Higher-role members nominate others. |
| 17 | **Build** | Collab space for building apps or protocols natively on Ritual. |
| 18 | **Support** | Open a private support ticket (upload logs and screenshots). |
| 19 | **Language Channels** | Section containing different language channels for Ritual supporters regardless of nationality. |
