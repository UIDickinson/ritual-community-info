# Ritual Community — Roles

After the NPC war and the 21-day challenge, the Ritual community underwent a full **Role Revamp**. Only Zealots in the Ritual Army retained their Ritualist role. The new system was designed to protect against future bot invasions.

## Active Roles

| Role                | Description |
|---------------------|-------------|
| **Radiant Ritualist** | Golden Ritualist. Super rare, reserved only for real community leaders. |
| **Ritualist**         | "Let the Ritual Begin 🕯️" — Awarded to followers and active community yappers. Stay active and the path will be revealed. |
| **Ritty**             | Long-term, loyal community member with conviction for what Ritual is building. Grants access to an exclusive Telegram chat. |
| **Ritty Bitty**       | A baby Ritualist. On the right path, recognized, but with a long way to go. |
| **Ascendant**         | You have pledged to Ritual. This is the start of your community journey, your first step. |
| **Initiate**          | You have just joined the server. |
| **Dunce**             | For those without intelligence. |
| **Node Runner**       | For early node runners. **No longer available.** |
| **Forerunner**        | From the time before Ritual. A limited number made it through the void. Given to early community members. |
| **Mage 🔮**          | You channel the arts and script to conjure highly impactful, creative contributions for Ritual. Given to exceptional artists on special occasions. |
| **Cursed**            | Your fellow community members have given you more curses than blessings. |
| **Blessed**           | Your fellow community members have given you more blessings than curses. |
| **Harmonic**          | You have equal blessings and curses. |

## Role Lore (NPC War Context)

> After the war against the NPC was over, the machine was defeated — but at what cost? During the epic battle, the NPC infiltrated the heart of Ritual. The community was overrun. They even transformed the event manager Kash into an NPC leader. In the aftermath of the epic 21-day challenge, the community had to revamp. Many were corrupted by the NPC scourge. All except Zealots lost the Ritualist role.
